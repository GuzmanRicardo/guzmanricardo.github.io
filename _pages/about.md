Hello ! I am a third-year PhD candidate at Aix-Marseille School of Economics (AMSE). I study how large-scale policies affect firms and markets in developing countries using tools from economic geography and spatial economics. My current research looks at the implications of industrial policy in agricultural technology markets on smallholder farms.
