Hello ! I am a second-year PhD candidate at the Aix-Marseille School of Economics (AMSE). I am broadly interested in firms and markets in in lower- and middle-income countries (LMICs). My current research looks at how large-scale policies (e.g. industrial policies, place-based subsidies) affect firm and household outcomes.
